The project-index code and layout are maintained in this repository. Its media assets come from the projects it indexes:

- `assets/usefultool.png`, `assets/neko-playlist.png`, `assets/boltzmann.png`, `assets/renju.png`, and `assets/tensor-playground.png` are screenshots generated from the corresponding browser applications.
- `assets/recorderlong.png` comes from `RecorderLong/art/app_icon_source.png`.
- `assets/studyshield.png` comes from `WafuStudyShield/app/src/main/assets/companion_packs/codex_cat_girl/character.png`.
- `assets/java-sobel.png` is output generated by the Java Tutorial lesson 4 Sobel demonstration.

No third-party runtime library or stock-image collection is used by the project index. Ownership and licensing of project-specific assets remain with their originating repositories and contributors.
